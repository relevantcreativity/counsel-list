About The Person(s) Involved

Biblical Counselor

Biblical counselors are Christians who help struggling people identify relevant principles from Scripture and apply them to particular situations or circumstances while providing personal spiritual coaching to promote enriched faith development.

Learn More

Mediators

Christian mediators help disputing parties apply principles from Scripture to achieve a voluntary resolution that is mutually agreeable while actively striving to reconcile and restore the affected relationship.

Learn More

Arbitrators

Christian arbitrators help disputing parties by considering relevant evidence in light of principles from Scripture before rendering a binding judgment that may be enforced in official courts of law. They also directly encourage the parties to reconcile and restore their relationship.

Learn More

Attorneys

Christian attorneys may provide specific legal advice, often specialize in particular areas of legal practice, may be licensed to practice in one or more states, and may represent clients in legal actions before official courts of law. They understand the importance of reconciling the contesting parties and recognize that our current American justice system utterly fails to address this critical relationship aspect.
